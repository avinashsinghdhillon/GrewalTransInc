Grewal Trans Inc. is a Smartway Certified freight carrier serving the United States and Canada. The company owns late model equipment to provide reliable and timely transportation for refrigerated and dry freight all year round. Our equipment complies with all CARB and FMCSA regulations.

Our headquarters and main terminal are located in Elk Grove, CA . Our regular lanes serve the North-West United States, in addition to BC and AB in Canada.
