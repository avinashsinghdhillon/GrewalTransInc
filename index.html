We are Grewal Trans, Inc., a transport company dedicated to providing the best transportation service in the area. With experienced drivers and well cared for, new equipment, we can deliver your shipments when and how you need it. From its inception in 2006, our mission has been to be a leader in freight transportation, providing fast, efficient and friendly service. Starting with only one truck in 2006, the company has expanded to keep pace with the rising need in the transportation industry. We currently have 25 trucks and 25 trailers. Grewal Trans can meet any transportation need by offering full load freight services throughout the continental United States and Canada.

Our headquarters and main terminal are located in Elk Grove, CA . The company owns late model equipment to provide reliable and timely transportation for refrigerated and dry freight all year round. Our equipment complies with all CARB and FMCSA regulations.
